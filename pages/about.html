Bio

Hannah is originally from Western Massachusetts and moved to Boston in 2018. She has worked at Boston University for three years in various administrative roles. She holds a Bachelor’s degree from the Isenberg School of Management of the University of Massachusetts Amherst in Operations and Information Management where she also minored in Information Technology. Currently she is pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Information Systems concentrating in Web Application Development. She is set to graduate in August of 2022.

In her free time, she enjoys taking classes, roller-skating, seeing what’s new on Netflix, and exploring the city.
In the snowy months, she spends her weekends snowboarding and enjoying hot chocolate.
